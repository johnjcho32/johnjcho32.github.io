Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at Yale University, working with Alan Gerber and Gregory Huber. I will be applying to PhD programs in Political Science this coming Fall.

I am broadly interested in questions in REP, ethnic minority political behavior, and elections.

I earned my A.B., summa cum laude, in Government (with High Honors) and Quantitative Social Sciences at Dartmouth College in 2022. I have also taken a wide variety of undergraduate/graduate classes in the Mathematics and Statistics Departments at Yale University from 2022-2024.
