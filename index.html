Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at Yale University, working with Alan Gerber and Gregory Huber. I earned my A.B., summa cum laude, in Government (with High Honors) and Quantitative Social Sciences at Dartmouth College in 2022.

I am broadly interested in questions in REP, ethnic minority political behavior, and elections.
